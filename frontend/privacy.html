Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information Naijamart Ltd ("Naijamart", "we") collects, how we use it, and the rights you have over your data.

1. Information we collect

  • Account data — name, email, phone number and password when you register.
  • Store data — products, prices, photos and inventory you upload.
  • Transaction data — order amounts, buyer messages and payout history.
  • Usage data — device, IP, browser and pages visited (cookies).

2. How we use it

  • To run your store and process orders.
  • To send transactional and product updates.
  • To prevent fraud and comply with Nigerian law (NDPR, CBN, EFCC).

3. Sharing

We never sell your data. We share only with payment processors, shipping partners, and authorities when legally required.

4. Your rights

You can access, correct, export, or delete your data at any time. Contact privacy@naijamart.ng.

5. Security

Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. Passwords are hashed with industry-standard algorithms.

6. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you signed in and analytics cookies to improve the product. You may disable cookies in your browser.

7. Changes

We'll notify you of material changes via email and update the date above.
